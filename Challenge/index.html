Challenge Plugin

The Challenge Plugin is a plugin with some cool challenges you can absolve alone or with your friends.


The plugin is still in development.

Download

Challenges

Block Drop Randomizer

Every block drops a random item when broken. For every player its an other drop.

Half Heart Challenge

You only have a half heart

Shared Inventory Challenge

All players has the same inventory

Damage equials inventory clear

When a player gets damage, the inventory from ALL players will be clearred.

Crafting Randomizer

Every crafting recipe gives a random item. For every player its an other result.

Block Drop is a random mob

When you break a block, a random mob will spawn. You will get the block drop when you kill the mob.

All Items

The goal is it to collect every Minecraft Item

All mobs

The goal is it to kill every Minecraft Mob

All Achievements

The goal is it to unlock every Minecraft Achievement
